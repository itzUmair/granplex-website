Through Granplex efforts of adding more and more screens, Pakistani producers overtime have gained the confidence to create more content.
Being at the pinnacle of technology, design and art, Granplex Cinemas is the standard by which all visual and aural presentation is judged. Our mission is simple, to provide the most remarkable cinema experience in the world right here in Pakistan. Granplex Cinemas is a revolution, an ambitious and challenging idea bought to life and a film lover's paradise.
